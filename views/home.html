Save the World Clicker!

Welcome to Save the U.S. Clicker. You’ve recently been appointed head of the US Department of Energy and now need to figure out a way to provide the population with energy while minimizing harm to the environment.

How to Play

The top of the page displays your stats such as population, CO2 level, and approval rating. You’ll campaign to raise money by clicking the large green button at the top of the page. It is important to make sure you don’t let the budget fall to 0! There are 6 different types of energy plants, some of which cause CO2 emissions. Without going over budget, you must purchase plants to generate the required energy. Every ten seconds, a month passes in game. Every month you must cover the costs of running the plant, so make sure you’re clicking the green bar!

Game Features

Build power plants and adjust how much of each energy source each assigned state will be producing
Experience more and more natural disasters as the planet's CO2 level increases
Invest in the country's future by building more green power plants
Control different states each that have different costs for different energy sources
Experience revolt from your citizens as you mismanage your country
